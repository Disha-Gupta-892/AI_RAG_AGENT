# TechCorp Employee Handbook - Remote Work Policy

## Remote Work Guidelines

Last Updated: January 2024

### Overview
TechCorp embraces flexible work arrangements to support work-life balance while maintaining productivity and collaboration. This policy outlines the guidelines for remote work eligibility and expectations.

### Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day probationary period are eligible for remote work arrangements, subject to manager approval and role requirements.

### Remote Work Options

1. **Hybrid Model (Standard)**
   - Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week
   - Minimum 2 days per week in office required
   - Core collaboration days: Tuesday and Thursday (in-office)

2. **Full Remote**
   - Available for specific roles approved by department heads
   - Requires quarterly in-person meetings
   - Must be within 4 hours of the main office timezone

### Equipment and Workspace

- Company provides laptop, monitor, and essential peripherals
- Home office stipend: $500 one-time setup allowance
- Monthly internet reimbursement: $50

### Communication Expectations

- Available during core hours: 10 AM - 3 PM local time
- Respond to messages within 2 hours during work hours
- Camera on for team meetings
- Daily check-in with team via Slack or Teams

### Performance Standards

Remote employees are held to the same performance standards as in-office employees. Performance is measured by:
- Meeting deadlines and deliverables
- Quality of work output
- Collaboration and communication
- Participation in team activities

### Security Requirements

- Use company VPN for all work activities
- Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts
- Never use public WiFi without VPN
- Report any security incidents immediately

### Approval Process

1. Discuss with direct manager
2. Submit Remote Work Request form in HR portal
3. Await approval (typically 5 business days)
4. Sign Remote Work Agreement

For questions, contact HR at hr@techcorp.com or extension 5500.
