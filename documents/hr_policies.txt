# TechCorp HR Policies - Leave and Benefits

## Employee Leave and Benefits Guide

Effective Date: January 2024 | Human Resources Department

### Types of Leave

#### 1. Paid Time Off (PTO)

PTO is based on years of service:

| Years of Service | Annual PTO Days |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| 0-3 years | 15 days |
| 4-7 years | 20 days |
| 8+ years | 25 days |

**PTO Guidelines:**
- Requests should be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance
- Maximum consecutive days: 10 (without VP approval)
- Unused PTO: Up to 5 days can roll over annually
- PTO is paid out upon separation (up to 40 hours)

#### 2. Sick Leave
- Unlimited sick leave policy (use responsibly)
- Doctor's note required for absences > 3 consecutive days
- Can be used for personal illness or family care
- No payout upon separation

#### 3. Parental Leave
- **Primary Caregiver**: 16 weeks paid leave
- **Secondary Caregiver**: 6 weeks paid leave
- Can be taken within 12 months of birth/adoption
- Gradual return option: 50% schedule for 2 additional weeks

#### 4. Bereavement Leave
- Immediate family: 5 paid days
- Extended family: 3 paid days
- Additional unpaid leave available upon request

#### 5. Jury Duty
- Full pay for jury service
- Must provide court documentation
- Return to work expected on non-service days

### Health Benefits

#### Medical Insurance
Three plan options available:
1. **PPO Select**: $150/month employee, $400/month family
2. **HDHP with HSA**: $75/month employee, $200/month family
3. **HMO Basic**: $100/month employee, $275/month family

TechCorp contributes to HSA accounts:
- Employee only: $500/year
- Family coverage: $1,000/year

#### Dental Insurance
- Preventive care: 100% covered
- Basic procedures: 80% covered
- Major procedures: 50% covered
- Annual maximum: $2,000

#### Vision Insurance
- Annual eye exam: fully covered
- Frames: $200 allowance every 2 years
- Contact lenses: $150 allowance annually

### Retirement Benefits

#### 401(k) Plan
- Immediate eligibility upon hire
- Company match: 100% on first 4%, 50% on next 2%
- Maximum company contribution: 5% of salary
- Immediate vesting on all contributions

#### ESPP (Employee Stock Purchase Plan)
- Purchase stock at 15% discount
- Contribute 1-15% of salary
- Enrollment periods: January and July

### Professional Development

#### Education Assistance
- Up to $5,000/year for approved courses
- Must maintain B grade or better
- Commitment to stay 1 year after completion

#### Conference Attendance
- Up to 2 conferences per year
- Budget: $3,000 annually
- Requires manager approval

### Wellness Programs

- Gym membership reimbursement: $50/month
- Mental health support: Free EAP counseling
- Annual health screening: Fully covered
- Wellness challenges with prizes
