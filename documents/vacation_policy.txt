# Vacation and Time Off Policy

Standard PTO: 15 days per year for the first 3 years.
Seniority Bonus: 20 days per year after 5 years of service.
Management: 25 days per year.
Sick Leave: 10 days per year (untracked, but requires notification).
Holydays: All federal holidays are observed.
PTO Rollover: Up to 5 days can be carried over to the next calendar year.
Unused PTO beyond 5 days will expire on December 31st.
Parental Leave: 12 weeks of paid leave for both parents.
Bereavement Leave: 3 days for immediate family members.
